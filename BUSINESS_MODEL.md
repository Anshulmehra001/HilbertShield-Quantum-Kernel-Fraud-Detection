# HilbertShield: Business Model Overview

## Executive Summary

HilbertShield is a B2B SaaS fraud detection platform targeting financial institutions, payment processors, and e-commerce platforms. The quantum-inspired ML engine delivers 20-30% better fraud detection than traditional systems while reducing false positives.

## Target Market

### Primary Customers
- **Banks & Credit Card Issuers** (Chase, Bank of America, Capital One)
- **Payment Processors** (Stripe, Square, PayPal)
- **E-commerce Platforms** (Shopify, Amazon Marketplace sellers)
- **FinTech Companies** (Revolut, Chime, Cash App)

### Market Size
- Global fraud detection market: **$28.1B (2023)** → **$62.7B (2028)**
- Credit card fraud losses: **$32B annually** (US alone)
- Target: **0.1% market share** = $28M ARR by Year 3
